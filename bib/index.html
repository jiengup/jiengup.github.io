About Me

I am currently a software engineer at Baidu AI Cloud, where I work on the research and development of industry-leading large-scale POSIX-compatible distributed cloud file storage and parallel file systems optimized for AI infrastructure. Before joining Baidu in June 2025, I received my M.S. in Computer Architecture from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), advised by Prof. Hua Wang and Prof. Ke Zhou. During my graduate studies, I was involved in several research projects and publications. I have also conducted research internships at Tencent Cloud and SenseTime, where I worked on the optimization of exabyte-scale cloud block systems and innovation in computer vision algorithms, respectively.

My current research interests lie in persistent and memory storage systems optimized for generative AI workloads, with a broader goal of contributing to the advancement of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).
